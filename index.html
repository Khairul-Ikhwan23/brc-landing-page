Our Story

For over 75 years, Brunei Red Crescent has been at the forefront of humanitarian assistance, serving the people of Brunei Darussalam with dedication and compassion.

1948

Brunei Red Cross Branch Established

The foundation of humanitarian work in Brunei begins with the establishment of the Red Cross branch.

1990

Incorporated as Brunei Darussalam Red Crescent

Official incorporation as a national society under the name Brunei Darussalam Red Crescent.

1996

Recognized by ICRC

Gained recognition from the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC).

1997

Admitted into IFRC

Became the 170th National Society admitted into the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC).

Today

Humanitarian Services Nationwide

Providing comprehensive humanitarian services across Brunei Darussalam with dedicated volunteers and staff.

What We Do

Our comprehensive range of humanitarian services addresses the diverse needs of our community, from emergency response to long-term support programs.

First Aid & Medical Coverage

Providing essential first aid training and medical coverage for events.

Psychological Support & Mental Health

Offering mental health support and psychological assistance.

Wellness & Road Safety Programs

Promoting wellness initiatives and road safety awareness.

BRC Care

Providing humanitarian aid, food assistance, and elderly care.

Emergency Response (CERT)

Community Emergency Response Team for disaster relief.

Volunteer Training

Comprehensive training programs for volunteers.
